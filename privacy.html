Privacy Policy

This website is a simple keyboard testing tool. We do not ask you to create an account or submit personal information.

Data we may collect

  • Anonymous usage data (if analytics is enabled): page views, device/browser type, and approximate location (city-level).
  • No key logs are sent: key presses are processed in your browser to highlight keys on the on-screen keyboard.

Cookies

If analytics or ads are enabled in the future, third-party cookies may be used for measurement and advertising. You can block cookies in your browser settings.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact the site owner via the repository page.
